Site Name

Agricultural product Sales website

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an online platform for selling Agricultural product such as;

  • Fertilizers
  • Pesticides
  • Seeds
  • Farm machinery
  • Animal feed

Scenarios

The site will be used by farmers, marketers, and anyone interested in buying or selling Agricultural product. They will come to the website with some of these questions in mind:

  • What kind of chemical could be use for some specific insect/pest control?
  • Prices of these chemicals?
  • How to buy these chemicals?
